# Extend Surface

<img width="200" height="200" alt="Extend Surface component icon" src="../../images/Extend_Surface_400.png" />

<div class="component-grid" markdown="1">

<div class="component-box box-desc" markdown="1">
Extends an existing surface outward from one or more of its edges by a specified distance or proportion. You can choose to extend specific edges or all sides simultaneously.
</div>

<div class="component-box box-menu" markdown="1">
## Menu Options

**Use Simple Domain Extension**  
Extends the surface proportionally rather than by a length
</div>

<div class="component-box box-in" markdown="1">
## Inputs

**Surface**  
The main surface

**Length**  
Length of the extension to the surface

**Edge**  
Choose which edge to extend
</div>

<div class="component-box box-out" markdown="1">
## Outputs

**Surface**  
The extended surface

**Notes**  
A description of how to use this tool
</div>

</div>
