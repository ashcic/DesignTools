# Centre Point

<img width="200" height="200" alt="Centre Point component icon" src="../../images/Centre_Point_400.png" />

<div class="component-grid" markdown="1">

<div class="component-box box-desc" markdown="1">
Finds the center points of input geometry, either by finding the individual centres of each piece of geometry, or by grouping them in to a union, and finding the overall centre point.
</div>

<div class="component-box box-menu" markdown="1">
## Menu Options

**Union**  
Treats the geometry as a group and returns one point

**Individual**  
Treats the geometry as individuals and returns multiple points
</div>

<div class="component-box box-in" markdown="1">
## Inputs

**Geometry**  
The main geometry
</div>

<div class="component-box box-out" markdown="1">
## Outputs

**Centers**  
The centre point of the geometry
</div>

</div>
