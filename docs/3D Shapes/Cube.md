# Cube

<img width="200" height="200" alt="Cube component icon" src="../../images/Cube_400.png" />

<div class="component-grid" markdown="1">

<div class="component-box box-desc" markdown="1">
Creates a 3D cube based on specified X, Y, and Z dimensions. Useful for 'Box Select'.

Includes an option to either center the cube on its midpoint or align it starting from its base.
</div>

<div class="component-box box-menu" markdown="1">
## Menu Options

**Centre**  
If true, the centre of the cube will be at the origin
If false, the base of the cube will be at the origin
</div>

<div class="component-box box-in" markdown="1">
## Inputs

**X**  
X Dimension

**Y**  
Y Dimension

**Z**  
Z Dimension
</div>

<div class="component-box box-out" markdown="1">
## Outputs

**cube**  
Final Cube
</div>

</div>
