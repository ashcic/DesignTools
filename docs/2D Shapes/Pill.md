Create a Pill or Race-track shape

___

### Inputs
**X**
The X dimension

**Y**
The Y dimension

**Orient**
The orientation of the Pill

___

### Outputs
**Curves**
Individual curves

**Joined**
Joined curves

**Pivots**
Pivot Points at the centres of the arcs
