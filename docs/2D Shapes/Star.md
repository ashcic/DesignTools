# Star

Creates a star shape defined by the outer and inner radii

___

### Inputs
**Radius A**
Outer construction circle radius

**Radius B**
Inner construction circle radius

**Number of Points**
Number of points

___

### Outputs
**Curves**
Individual curves

**Joined**
Joined curves

**Points**
Control Points
