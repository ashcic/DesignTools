# Star

<img width="200" height="200" alt="Star component icon" src="../../images/Star_400.png" />


Creates a star shape by defining two circles, one on which creates the outside points, the other creates the inside valleys.

___

## Inputs

**Radius A**
Outer construction circle radius

**Radius B**
Inner construction circle radius

**Number of Points**
Number of points

___

## Outputs

**Curves**
Individual curves

**Joined**
Joined curves

**Points**
Control Points
