# Star

<img width="200" height="200" alt="Star component icon" src="../../images/Star_400.png" />

<div class="component-grid" markdown="1">

<div class="component-box box-desc" markdown="1">
Creates a star shape by defining two circles, one on which creates the outside points, the other creates the inside valleys.
</div>

<div class="component-box box-in" markdown="1">
## Inputs

**Radius A**  
Outer construction circle radius

**Radius B**  
Inner construction circle radius

**Number of Points**  
Number of points
</div>

<div class="component-box box-out" markdown="1">
## Outputs

**Curves**  
Individual curves

**Joined**  
Joined curves

**Points**  
Control Points
</div>

</div>
